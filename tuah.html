Hawk Named Tuah
Tuah is a species of Red-tailed hawk indigenous to Utah named after the Malaysian folk hero Hang Tuah, who was a great warrior and admiral under Sultan Mansur Shah's empire. These new, yet still largely unseen, documents and footage reveal that Tuah the Red-tailed Hawk was the original inspiration for the infamous "Hawk Tuah" meme, and was even often said to "spit on that thang". While this information is still yet to become viral, this meme token has taken advantage of ground-breaking knowledge to give recognition credit to itself and by its discovery, not only break the internet with the unknown origin of the infamous meme, but also bring enough attention to itself to become the next great meme token.
